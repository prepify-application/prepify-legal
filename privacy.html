Prepify Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 3, 2026

Prepify ("the app", "we") is a health and self-improvement app: nutrition tracking, AI meal and workout planning, habit tracking and to-dos. This policy explains what data we collect, why, and what your rights are. The short version: we collect what the app needs to work, we don't sell your data, and you can delete everything yourself at any time from inside the app.

1. Data we collect

DataWhy we collect it
Email address and password (or your Google/Apple account identity)To create and secure your account
Profile details: name, age, sex, height, weight, goal weight, activity level, goalsTo calculate your personal calorie and macro targets
Dietary requirements (e.g. allergies, halal, vegan) including free-text notesTo generate meal plans that respect your requirements
Training profile: experience level, training days per week, session length, available equipment, injury areas and free-text injury notes, training goalTo generate workout plans that fit your schedule and equipment and avoid exercises that conflict with the injuries you report
Food diary entries, saved meals, weight log, generated meal plansThis is the app's core function — tracking what you eat and plan
Generated workout plans and your workout logs (sessions, exercises completed, and any sets, reps and weights you choose to record)To show your training week and track your progress
Habits you add (including the names of custom habits), your completion and skip history, and any reminder times you setTo track your habits, streaks and reminders
Tasks: title, description, due date and reminder timeTo keep your to-do list and remind you about it
Barcodes you scanSent to the OpenFoodFacts database to look up nutrition info. We do not store camera images; the camera is used only live, on your device, to read the barcode.
Subscription statusTo know whether Premium features are unlocked

We do not collect your precise location, contacts, or photos, and we do not sell personal data to anyone. Habit and task reminders are local notifications scheduled on your device — we do not send push notifications through our servers. App preferences such as light/dark theme are stored only on your device. The free tier shows ads (banner and occasional full-screen) served by Google AdMob (see section 3); Premium subscribers see no ads.

2. Health-related data

Your weight, goals, dietary requirements, food logs, training profile — including any injuries you report — and workout logs are health-related information. We treat them as sensitive: they are stored with row-level security so only your authenticated account can read them, and they are used solely to provide the app's features described above.

3. Who processes your data

ServiceRole
SupabaseHosts our database and authentication. All your app data lives here.
Google Gemini APIGenerates your weekly meal and workout plans. For meal plans we send your nutrition targets, meals-per-day preference and dietary requirements; for workout plans we send your training profile, including your injury notes. We never send your name, email or account identity.
OpenFoodFactsReceives the barcode number you scan and returns the product's nutrition info.
RevenueCat, Apple App Store / Google PlayProcess subscription purchases. We never see your payment card details.
Google AdMobServes ads (banner and occasional full-screen) to free-tier users only. AdMob may use a device advertising identifier; where required (e.g. EU/UK), you are shown a consent form and can choose non-personalised ads. Premium removes ads entirely.

4. How long we keep data

For as long as your account exists. When you delete your account (More → Delete Account), your profile, diary, weight logs, saved meals, meal plans, training profile, workout plans and logs, habits, tasks and authentication record are permanently erased immediately. Reminders scheduled on your device are cancelled by the app when you sign out or delete your account.

5. Your rights

Depending on where you live (e.g. GDPR in the EU/UK, CCPA in California), you have the right to access, correct, export, or delete your personal data, and to object to processing. Deletion is built into the app; for anything else, contact us and we will respond within 30 days.

6. Children

Prepify is not intended for children under 13 (or the minimum age in your country), and we do not knowingly collect data from them.

7. Security

Data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS) and protected at rest by per-user row-level security. No method of storage is 100% secure, but access to your data requires your authenticated session.

8. Changes

If we change this policy materially, we'll update the effective date above and note it in the app.

9. Contact

Questions or data requests: prepifys@outlook.com
